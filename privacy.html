Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Uni Companion. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled within our academic management platform.

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number on our servers.

2. User Data Management

The application stores the following data exclusively on your local device:

  • Academic GPA metrics (courses, grades, credits)
  • Lecture schedules and daily routines
  • Notes, reminders, and calendar events
  • Uploaded academic files (PDF, Excel, JSON)

3. Local File Processing

All file processing, including schedule imports, occurs locally on your hardware. No files are ever transmitted to external servers.

4. Analytics & Performance

We utilize Firebase Analytics to monitor app performance and stability. This includes technical device data and anonymized usage behavior to help us improve the user experience.

5. Data Storage & Security

By keeping your data stored locally, we significantly minimize security risks. You retain full ownership and can delete all application data at any time via the internal settings menu.
